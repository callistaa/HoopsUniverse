Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do I have to be members of HOOPS UNIVERSE to buy a products?

No. You can just choose any of products that you want, then fill the validation form to give your delivery information.

How do I buy products from HOOPS UNIVERSE?

Use the menu on the top of the page to browse by product category. To access more detail or purchase an item, you can also click on the product image and you will be taken to the item's product detail page as well as to validate your purchase. When you're ready to complete your order, be sure to review all of the items. Once you have all the items you wish to purchase, click the submit button.

What are HOOPS UNIVERSE online payment method?

You can pay with any of Credit Cards (Visa or MasterCard) , Debit Cards (Visa or MasterCard), and PayPal.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, We do. We have received requests for our products from around the world and we are happy to now be able to provide an international shipping solution. Please carefully read our procedures to learn more about how our international shipping works.

Can I cancel my order?

No, you can't. Once an order has been processed, we are unable to cancel it or make any changes to the order or shipping address.

Can I have a refund?

Yes you can. But you should contact our customer service and the refund will be processed according to the terms and conditions that apply.
